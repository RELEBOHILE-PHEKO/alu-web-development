ALU Software Engineering

Welcome to the African Leadership University's Bachelor of Software Engineering program.

Modules

Intro to Linux and IT Tools, Python Programming, and Databases are the modules introduced in the second trimester of the program. In the third trimester, we have Web Development and Web Infrastructure.

Foundation Modules

These modules equip you with extensive knowledge and expertise, especially in working in a group or team and developing leadership skills. We have Self Leadership and Team Dynamics, and Responsible Enterprise. The Self Leadership and Team Dynamics module is taught in the second trimester, while Responsible Enterprise is taught in the third trimester, which is the last trimester of the first year.
